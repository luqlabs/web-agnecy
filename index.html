Pertanyaan Umum

Semua yang ingin Anda tahu tentang bekerja sama dengan kami. 

Berapa lama proses pengerjaan website?

No hard feelings. You can pause or cancel anytime. We’re not that clingy. But we bet you’ll miss us.

Layanan apa saja yang LuqLabs tawarkan?

We also do marketing — the good kind that makes people click, buy, or DM you. Design’s our first love, but marketing’s our side hustle (that we’re really good at).

Apakah website yang dibuat mobile-friendly?

As many as your heart desires. We work on one at a time (to keep it spicy and focused). Queue ‘em up, we’ll knock ‘em down.

Apakah saya bisa minta revisi?

It’s super simple. You pay a flat fee every month, and we become your on-demand design (or design + dev) team. Send as many tasks as you want, we work through them one at a time. No contracts. No weird commitments. Pause or cancel anytime, we won’t take it personally.

Bagaimana cara memulai project bersama LuqLabs?

You won’t. But if you do, we’ll redo it. And redo it again. Until you feel that “Yup, that’s it” moment.

Apakah LuqLabs juga menangani SEO?

Ya! Setiap website yang kami buat sudah dilengkapi dengan SEO basic (meta title, description, struktur heading yang benar, kecepatan loading). Untuk SEO lanjutan, kami juga bisa membantu.

Mengapa memilih LuqLabs dibanding agency lain?

We actually reply (ASAP). We don’t hide behind “let’s touch base” emails. And we make brands look fresh, not like everyone else.
